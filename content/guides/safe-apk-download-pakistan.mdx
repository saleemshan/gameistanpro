## Unknown sources toggle

Enable **Install unknown apps** only for your browser or file manager, then disable it after installing.

## Compare metadata

Match **version**, **size**, and **permissions** to our listing pages before you open the package.

## Second device rule

When possible, test new earning APKs on a **spare handset** without banking apps logged in.

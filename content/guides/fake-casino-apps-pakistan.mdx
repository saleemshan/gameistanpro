## Start with the publisher trail

Clone apps often reuse **similar icons** and miss long-term update history. Compare the package label on-device with the publisher statement on this site.

## Payment safety

Never send **CNIC photos** or **OTP codes** to WhatsApp “agents.” Legit wallets bill inside verified flows.

## When to walk away

If an APK demands upfront PKR transfers before you can withdraw, treat it as high risk and cross-check community reports.

## How withdrawal flows usually work (editorial model)

Most third-party **real-money style** apps in Pakistan follow a loose pattern:

1. **In-app balance** accumulates from play, tasks, or promos (varies wildly by publisher).
2. You open a **Withdraw** or **Wallet** screen and choose **JazzCash** or **EasyPaisa**.
3. You enter **account details** tied to **your own** wallet—not a stranger’s.
4. The publisher’s backend may run **fraud checks**, causing delays.

We do not operate these flows—always read the **in-app** rules for the specific APK you installed.

## Common mistakes (avoid these)

- Binding a **friend’s** wallet “for faster payout.”
- Paying an **“agent fee”** in advance to “release” a stuck withdrawal.
- Sharing **OTP + PIN** together in WhatsApp “support.”
- Assuming our editorial pages **guarantee** a payout timeline—timelines are publisher-specific.

Roman Urdu line: **Withdrawal sirf apne wallet number par—dusron ka account use na karein.**

## Delay reasons (non-exhaustive)

- **KYC / limits** on JazzCash or EasyPaisa tiers.
- **Risk review** after rapid deposit/withdraw cycles.
- **Maintenance windows** announced inside the wallet app.
- **Publisher backlog** during promos (e.g., Ramadan campaigns—see also [Ramzan packages](/guides/ramzan-packages)).

## Troubleshooting a “stuck” withdrawal

1. Capture **timestamp**, **amount**, and any **reference ID** shown in-app.
2. Use **official in-app support** channels only.
3. Check wallet **notifications** for declined reasons.
4. If someone asks for an OTP to “speed it up,” treat that as **compromised support**—stop.

## Protecting wallet details

- Keep **JazzCash / EasyPaisa** apps updated.
- Enable **app lock** / biometric where available.
- Avoid screen-sharing your wallet while a “helper” watches.

## Link accounts deliberately

Only connect wallets through **in-app menus** you can trace back to the publisher APK you installed.

## SMS phishing

Banks and wallets will not ask for **full OTP + PIN** in chat apps. Report suspicious threads.

## Tax and limits

Withdrawal limits change by KYC tier—confirm inside JazzCash / EasyPaisa before moving large PKR amounts.
